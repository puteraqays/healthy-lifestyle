Health Tips

Stay Hydrated

Drinking enough water helps regulate body temperature, improve concentration, and maintain overall health. Adults should aim to drink at least 2 litres of water daily to stay hydrated and support body functions.

Exercise Regularly

Regular physical activity improves cardiovascular health, strengthens muscles, and reduces the risk of chronic diseases. Aim for at least 30 minutes of exercise each day to maintain a healthy lifestyle.

Healthy Diet

A balanced diet includes fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Proper nutrition helps boost immunity, maintain energy levels, and support overall physical and mental well-being.
